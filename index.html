FUNCION VIDEO 2009

FUNCION VIDEO – Festival de minimetrages was the first film festival in Spain to promote the use of mobile devices and digital cameras for short films production.

Details

Details

Back in 2006, emerging media such as mobile phones and digital cameras changed users’ approach to photography and film production. For first time, an accessible tool let any and all to produce movies. To give these new filmmakers a place in the audiovisual scene CP created FV.

Details

Over four years, from 2006 to 2009, FV promoted the creative use of mobile devices. The success of this idea was reflected in the high participation from all Europe and South America. The use that the participants did of the tiny cameras of their personal device was astonishedly creative and ingenious.

Details

Details

Each year the best productions were screened in the city of Barcelona and prizes were awarded to the winners of each sections: Documentary, Narrative and Video Art.
